There was a man in the land of Counsel (Uz), Persecuted (Job) his name;
and that man was whole and straight, 
and fearing God, and turning-aside from bad. 
And it came to be the day, 
and the sons of God came to position themselves over He-Who-Is (YHWH).

And it was in that day, 
and the Adversary (Satan) also came in the midst of the sons of God, 
and He-Who-Is (YHWH) said to the Adversary (Satan) "from where do you come?" 
And the Adversary (Satan) answered He-Who-Is (YHWH), 
and said, "from roving in the land, and from walking in it."

And He-Who-Is (YHWH) said to the Adversary (Satan), 
"Have you placed your heart over my servant Persecuted (Job), 
that there is none like him in the land, a man whole and straight, 
fearing God, and turning-aside from bad?"

And the Adversary (Satan) answered He-Who-Is (YHWH), 
and said, "Has Persecuted (Job) feared God for-nothing?"

Have you not hedged around him and around his house, and all which is to him from all-around?

You have blessed the work of his hands, and you caused to-be-fruitful the abundance of his land. and indeed-but please send-out your hand, and touch in all which is to him;

if not he will bless you over your face. 
And He-Who-Is (YHWH) said to the Adversary (Satan), 
"Behold all which is to him is in your hand;
only to him do not send-out your hand."

And the Adversary (Satan) went out from being with the face of He-Who-Is (YHWH).
