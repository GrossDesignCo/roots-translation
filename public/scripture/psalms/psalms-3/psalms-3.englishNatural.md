A melody to David, in his fleeing from the face of Father-of-Peace (Absalom), his son. 
He-Who-Is (YHWH), what have been-many my enemies;
many ones-standing-up over me. 
Many say to my throat,
"No salvation to him in God." Selah. And you, He-Who-Is (YHWH), are a shield around me;
my glory and the one-raising my head. I call-out,
to He-Who-Is (YHWH) with my voice and he answers me from the mountain of his holiness Selah. 
I have lain-down and I slept;
I have awoken, that He-Who-Is (YHWH) supports me. I will fear 
not from ten-thousands of people, which all-around they have appointed over me. Stand-up, He-Who-Is (YHWH)! Save me, my God;
that you have struck-down all my enemies on the cheek;
you have broken the teeth of the wicked. To He-Who-Is (YHWH) the salvation; over your people, your blessing. Selah.
