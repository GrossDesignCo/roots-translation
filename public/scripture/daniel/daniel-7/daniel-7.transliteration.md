
aneh Daniel veAmar chazeh haveit beKhezvi im-leila 
vaAru arba ruchei shemaya 
meGichan leYamma rabba

veArba kheyvan ravravan salqan min-yamma 
shanyan da min-da

qadmayeta keAryeh veGappin di-neshar lah 
chazeh haveit ad di-meritu gappayh 
uNetilat min-ara veAl-raglayyin keEnash hoqimat 
uLevav enash yehiv lah

vaAru kheyva ochori tinyannah damyah leDov 
veListar-chad hoqimat 
uTelat ilin bePummah beyn shinnah 
veKhen amrin lah qumi akhul besar saggi

batar denah chazeh haveit vaAru ochori kiNmar 
veLah-gappin arba di-oph al-gabbah 
veArbea reshin lekheyveta 
veSholtan yehiv lah

batar denah chazeh haveit beKhezevei leila 
vaAru kheyva reviaa dekhilah veEmtani veTaqqipha yattirah 
veShinnayin di-parzel lah 
akhelah uMaddeqah uShara beRagleyh raphsah 
veHi meshanyah min-kol-kheyvata di qodamah 
veQarnayin asar lah

mistakkal haveit beQarnaiya 
vaAlu qeren ochori zeeirah silqat beynehon 
uTelat min-qarnaiya qadmayata iteqaru min-qodameyh 
vaAlu ayinin keAynei anasha beQarna-da 
uPum memallil ravrevan

chazeh haveit ad di korsavan remiw 
veAttiq yomin yetiv 
levusheh keTlag khivvar uSear reisheh kaAmar neqe 
korseyeh shevivin di-nur galgellohi nur daliq

nehar di-nur naged veNafeq min-qodamohi 
eleph alphin yeShammeshunneh veRibbo ribvan qodamohi yequmun 
dina yetiv veSiphrin pethichu

chazeh haveit beKhezevei leila 
vaAru im-ananei shemaya keVar enash 
ateh havah veAd-Attiq yomaya meta uQodamohi haqrevuhi
