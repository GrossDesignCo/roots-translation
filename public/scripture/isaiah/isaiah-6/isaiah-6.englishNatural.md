In the year of the death of the king, He-my-Strength (Uzziah).

And I saw my Lord sitting over a throne, high and lifted-up, 
and his garments, filling the temple.

Incinerating-ones were standing above him; six wings to each one, in two he covers his faces, and in two he covers his feet, and in two he flies.

And this called-out to this, and said, 

"Holy, holy, holy, He-Who-Is (YHWH) of Hosts,
fullness of all the land is his glory."

And pillars of the thresholds shook from the voice of the caller, and the house was filled with smoke.

And I said, "Woe to me that I am silenced; 
that I, a man of unclean lips, 
and in the midst of a people of unclean lips I sit; 
that my eyes have seen the King, He-Who-Is (YHWH) of Hosts."


And flew to me one from the incinerating-ones 
and in his hand a live-coal he had taken with tongs from over the altar.

And he touched it to my mouth,
and he said, "Behold, this has touched over your lips; 
and turned-aside your twisting, 
and your sin will be covered." And I heard the voice of my Lord saying, "whom will I send and who will walk to us?"

And I said, "behold-me; send me."
And he said, "Walk, and you will say to this people, Hear hearing, and you will understand not; and see seeing, and you will know
not." Make-fat the heart of this people, and make-heavy his ears, and seal-shut
his eyes; or-else he sees in his eyes, and hear in his ears, and his heart understands, and he returns, and have healed to him.
And I said, "Until when my Lord?"
And he said, "Until which cities are desolate without sitting, and houses without a human, and the ground is desolate, a desolation.
And He-Who-Is (YHWH) will remove-far the human, the leaving and great, in the midst of the land.
And again in her a tenth, and it will return, and will be to burn: like a terebinth and like an oak, which in casting-off a stump in them; a seed of holiness, her stump."
