
veAchar bau Moshe veAharon vaYomru el-Paroh koh-amar YHWH elohei Yisrael shallach et-ammi veYachogu li baMidbar

vaYomer Paroh mi YHWH asher eshma beQolo leshalach et-Yisrael lo yadati et-YHWH veGam et-Yisrael lo ashalach

vaYomru elohei haIvrim niqra aleinu nelkhah na derekh sheloshet yamim baMidbar veNizbechah laYHWH Eloheinu pen-yifgaenu baDever o veCherev

vaYomer alehem melekh Mitzrayim lamah Moshe veAharon tafriu et-haAm miMaasav lekhu leSivlotekhem

vaYomer Paroh hen-rabbim atah am haAretz veHishbatem otam miSivlotam
