
And-after came Drawn_Out (Moses) and-Light_Bringer (Aaron) and-said to- Pharaoh, "thus- said He_Who_Is (YHWH), Gods-of Struggles_with_God (Israel), 'send ↳ people-my and-will-feast to-me in-wilderness.'"

And-said Pharaoh "who He_Who_Is (YHWH) which I-will-hear in-voice-his to-send ↳ Struggles_with_God (Israel), not I-knew ↳ He_Who_Is (YHWH), and-also ↳ Struggles_with_God (Israel) not I-will-send."

And-they-said "Gods-of the-Passes_Through (Hebrews) was-called_out over-us, we-will-go please way-of three-of days in-wilderness and-we-will-sacrifice to-He_Who_Is (YHWH) Gods-our, or-else- he-will-strike_down-us in-the-plague or in-the-sword."

And-said to-them king-of Double_Narrows (Egypt) "why Drawn_Out (Moses) and-Light_Bringer (Aaron) will-you-cause-to-let_loose ↳ the-people from-works-their? go to-burdens-your."

And-said Pharaoh "behold- many now people-of the-land and-will-cause_to_sabbath ↳ from-burdens-their."
