
And it was in the half of the night, 
and He-Who-Is (YHWH) struck all firstborn in the land of Double-Narrows (Egypt), 
from the firstborn of the sitting Pharaoh over his throne, 
until the firstborn of the captive which, in the house of the pit, 
and all the firstborn of beast.

And Pharaoh arose, night, he and all his servants and all Double-Narrows (Egypt), 
and a great outcry was in Double-Narrows (Egypt), 
that there was not a house which there was not there a dead one.
