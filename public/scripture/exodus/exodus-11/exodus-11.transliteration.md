
vaYomer YHWH el-Moshe od nega echad avi al-Paroh veAl-Mitzrayim acharei-ken yeShalach etkhem mizeh keShalcho kalah garesh yegaresh etkhem mizeh

daber-na beOzney haAm veYishalu ish meEt reehu veIshah meEt reutah keley-kesef uKhley zahav

vaYitten YHWH et-chen haAm beEyney Mitzrayim gam haIsh Moshe gadol meod beEretz Mitzrayim beEyney avdey-Paroh uVeEyney haAm

vaYomer Moshe koh amar YHWH kaChatzot haLaylah ani yotse beTokh Mitzrayim

uMet kol-bekhor beEretz Mitzrayim miBekhor Paroh haYoshev al-kiso ad bekhor haShifchah asher achar haRechayim veKhol bekhor behemah

veHayetah tzaakah gedolah beKhol-eretz Mitzrayim asher kamohu lo nihyetah veKhamohu lo tosif

uLekhol beney Yisrael lo yecharatz-kelev leshono meIsh veAd-behemah lemaan tedun asher yafleh YHWH beyn Mitzrayim uVeyn Yisrael

veYardu kol-avadekha eleh elay veHishtachavu-li lemor tse atah veKhol-haAm asher-beRagleykha veAcharey-ken etse vaYetse meIm-Paroh boChori-af

vaYomer YHWH el-Moshe lo-yishma aleykhem Paroh lemaan rebot-moftay beEretz Mitzrayim

uMoshe veAharon asu et-kol-haMoftim haEleh lifney Paroh vayChazzeq YHWH et-lev Paroh veLo-shillach et-beney-Yisrael meArtzo
