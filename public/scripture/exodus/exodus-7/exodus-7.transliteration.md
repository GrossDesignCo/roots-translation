
vaYomer YHWH el-Moshe reeh netaticha Elohim leParoh veAharon achicha yihyeh neviekha

atah tedaber et kol-asher atzavekha veAharon achicha yedaber el-Paroh veShilach et-benei Yisrael meArtzo

vaAni aqsheh et-lev Paroh veHirbeti et-ototai veEt-moftai beEretz Mitzrayim

vaYomer YHWH el-Moshe veEl-Aharon lemor

ki yedaber alekhem Paroh lemor tenu lakhem mofet veAmarta el-Aharon qach et-matekha veHashlekh lifnei-Paroh yehi leTannin

vaYavo Moshe vAharon el-Paroh vaYaasu khen kaAsher tzivah YHWH vaYashlekh Aharon et-mattehu lifnei Paroh veLifnei avadav vaYehi leTannin

vaYiqra gam-Paroh laChakhamim veLaMkhashshefim vaYaasu gam-hem chartumei Mitzrayim beLahateihem khen

vaYashlikhu ish mattehu vaYihyu leTanninim 
vaYivla mateh-Aharon et-mattotam

vaYechezaq lev Paroh 
veLo shama alehem kaAsher diber YHWH
