And God spoke all the words, these, to say:
I, He-Who-Is (YHWH) your God, which I have brought-out you from the land of Double-Narrows (Egypt), from the house of servants.
Other gods will be not to you over my faces.
You will make not to you a carved-image and any likeness,
which is in the skies from above, and which is in the land from beneath, and which is in the waters from beneath to the land.
You will bow-down not to them and you will serve them
not. That I He-Who-Is (YHWH) your God, a God jealous;
visiting the iniquity of fathers over sons, over third-ones and over fourth-ones to ones hating me.
And making steadfast-love to thousands, to ones loving me and to ones keeping my commandments.
You will lift-up not the name of He-Who-Is (YHWH) your God to the emptiness;
that He-Who-Is (YHWH) will be-clean not which will lift-up his name to the emptiness.
Remember the day of the Sabbath, to sanctify it.
Six days you will serve, and you will make all your work.
And the day the seventh is a Sabbath to He-Who-Is (YHWH) your God.
You will make not any work:
you, and your son, and your daughter, your servant, and your maidservant, and your beast, and your sojourner which is in your gates.
That in six days has made He-Who-Is (YHWH) the skies and the land, the sea and all which is in them;
and he rested in the day the seventh.
Therefore has blessed He-Who-Is (YHWH) the day of the Sabbath and sanctified it.
Be-heavy upon your father and your mother;
for the sake your days will be-long over the ground which He-Who-Is (YHWH) your God is giving to you.
You will murder not.
You will commit-adultery not.
You will steal not.
You will answer not as a witness of falsehood against your friend.
You will desire not the house of your friend.
You will desire not the woman of your friend, and his servant and his maidservant and his ox and his donkey, and all which is to your friend.
