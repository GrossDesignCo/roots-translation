
vaYchazzeq YHWH et-lev Paroh 
veLo shillach et-benei Yisrael

vaYomer YHWH el-Moshe neteh yadkha al-haShamayim viYhi choshekh al-eretz Mitzrayim veYamesh choshekh

vaYet Moshe et-yado al-haShamayim 
vaYhi choshekh-afelah beKhol-eretz Mitzrayim sheloshet yamim

lo-rau ish et-achiv 
veLo-qamu ish miTachtav sheloshet yamim 
uLeKhol-benei Yisrael hayah or beMoshvotam

vaYiqra Paroh el-Moshe 
vaYomer lekhu ivdu et-YHWH 
raq tzonkhem uVqarkhem yutzag 
gam-tapkhem yelekh imakhem

vaYomer Moshe gam-atah titten beYadenu zevachim veOlot veAsinu laYHWH Eloheinu

veGam-miqnenu yelekh imanu 
lo tishaer parsah 
ki mimenu niqqach laAvod et-YHWH Eloheinu 
vaAnachnu lo-neda mah-naavod et-YHWH ad-boenu shamah

vaYchazzeq YHWH et-lev Paroh 
veLo avah leshalcham

vaYomer lo Paroh lekh meAlay hiShamer lekha al-tosef reot panay ki beYom reotekha panay tamut

vaYomer Moshe ken dibarta lo-osif od reot panekha
