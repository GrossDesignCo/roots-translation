
And-intensified He_Who_Is (YHWH) ↳ heart-of Pharaoh; 
and-not sent_out ↳ sons-of Struggles_with_God (Israel).

And-said He_Who_Is (YHWH) to- Drawn_Out (Moses) stretch_out hand-your over- the-skies and-let-be darkness over- land-of Double_Straits (Egypt) and-let-him-feel darkness."

And-stretched_out Drawn_Out (Moses) ↳ hand-his over- the-skies; 
and-was darkness-of gloom in-all-of- land-of Double_Straits (Egypt) three-of days.

not- saw man ↳ brother-his 
and-not- stood_up man from-lowness-his three-of days; 
and-to-all- sons-of Struggles_with_God (Israel) it-was light in-seats-their.

And-called_out Pharaoh to- Drawn_Out (Moses) 
and-said go serve ↳ He_Who_Is (YHWH); 
only flock-your and-herd-your will-be_stationed; 
also- little_ones-your will-go with-you.

And-said Drawn_Out (Moses) also- you will-give in-hand-our sacrifices and-ascending_offerings and-will-make to-He_Who_Is (YHWH) God-our.

and-also- livestock-our will-go with-us; 
not will-remain hoof; 
that from-it will-take to-serve ↳ He_Who_Is (YHWH) God-our; 
and-we not- will-know what- will-serve ↳ He_Who_Is (YHWH) until- coming-our there-ward.

And-intensified He_Who_Is (YHWH) ↳ heart-of Pharaoh; 
and-not was_willing to-send_out-them.

And-said to-him Pharaoh, "Go from-over-me; guard to-you, not add, to-see faces-my, that, in-day seeing-you faces-my, you-will-die."

And-said Drawn_Out (Moses), so you-spoke; not I-will-add again, to-see faces-your."
